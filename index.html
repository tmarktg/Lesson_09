Reading Manga

I recently fell in love with Manga again, and I have been reading this series called Oshi no Ko. It's a really emotional manga, and often the sad ones are the best because they display so much depth of emotion.

This manga tells the story of a woman who never experienced love before. She was abandoned by her mother and placed into a foster home. Eventually, she gets discovered by a scout who asks if she wants to become an idol. It's really interesting to see stories where people find opportunities by being in the right place at the right time.
