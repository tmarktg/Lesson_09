About me again!

This is a great place to talk about my hobbies. I love watching fantasy/horror movies on my free time, especially when I spend a lot of time on the computer for work. I also love running, well maybe the idea of running given it sucks while I am running.

Here are some more hobbies I have:

Trail Hiking: I hiked in Arizona recently, and I had a lot of fun on the steep rocks and getting a great view. The photo here is of Subway Cove in Arizona Sedona, and it is quite the steep hike up. There are two ways, one is slippery and steep, and the other one involves scaling a mountain a bit. We scaled the mountain.

Reading Manga: I recently fell into Manga again, and I have been reading this manga named Oshi no Ko, it's a really sad manga, unfortunately the sad ones are often the best ones because it displays so much emotion.

This manga is about a woman who never experienced love before, as she was abandoned by her mother and placed into a foster home. Eventually, she gets discovered by a scout who asks if she wants to become an idol. It's really interesting to see movies where people find opportunities at being at the right place at the right time.

Boxing: I have been boxing for 6 months now, and I enjoy going every day to practice and slowly improving my body. Although I do have to take some days off so that I don't exhaust my body too much before work.

Meeting new people: I have always been a pretty shy person, but I always love meeting new people, as it is sometimes isolating to be in a field where you spend 8 hours alone a day working on a laptop on a website.
